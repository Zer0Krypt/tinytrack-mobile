TinyTrack

One-time mobile setup — takes about 3 minutes

1
Install Certificate

TinyTrack uses a secure local connection. Install this certificate so your phone trusts it.

Download Certificate →
  1. Tap Download Certificate above — a file download prompt will appear
  2. Go to Settings → General → VPN & Device Management
  3. Tap TinyTrack Local CA and tap Install
  4. Go to Settings → General → About → Certificate Trust Settings
  5. Enable full trust for TinyTrack Local CA
2
Connect to Home Server

Your home server address is shown below. Open TinyTrack on your phone, go to Settings → Home Server, and enter this address.

Loading…

Or scan the QR code in the desktop app tray to open TinyTrack with the address pre-filled.

3
Add to Home Screen

Open TinyTrack in Safari, then add it to your home screen so it works like a real app.

  1. Tap the Share button ⬆ at the bottom of Safari
  2. Scroll down and tap Add to Home Screen
  3. Tap Add in the top-right corner
4
Enable Offline Mode

Open TinyTrack from your Home Screen icon once while on home WiFi.

  1. Open TinyTrack from your Home Screen icon (not from Safari)
  2. Stay on the app for about 10–15 seconds
  3. You'll see a small message: "App ready for offline use"

Once you see that message, TinyTrack will open instantly even when away from home.

That's it — one-time setup complete.
